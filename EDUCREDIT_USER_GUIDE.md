## Overview

**EduCredit** is a comprehensive education account system designed for Singapore Citizens aged 16-30. The system enables users to:
- Access education funds
- Pay for approved course fees
- Track their learning journey
- View transaction history and account balance

**Tagline:** *Empowering Education for Every Singaporean*

## How It Works

The EduCredit system operates in three simple steps:

### Step 1: Automatic Activation
- Your education account is **automatically created when you turn 16**
- No application required
- Available to all eligible Singapore Citizens

### Step 2: Receive Credits
- **Periodic top-ups** are credited to your account
- Credits are based on government schemes
- Automatic processing ensures timely funding

### Step 3: Pay Course Fees
- Use your account balance to pay for approved courses
- Multiple payment methods supported
- Seamless integration with educational institutions

### Dashboard Overview

Upon logging in, users are greeted with a comprehensive dashboard that serves as the central hub for all account activities. The dashboard is designed with user experience in mind, providing quick access to essential information and functions.

#### Dashboard Layout

**Top Navigation Bar**
- EduCredit logo (home link)
- Navigation menu items
- User profile icon with dropdown menu
- Notification bell icon
- Logout button

**Main Dashboard Area**
The dashboard is divided into several key sections:

1. **Account Summary Card** (Top Section)
   - Large, prominent display of current balance
   - Visual indicators for account status (Active/Inactive)
   - Account holder name and ID number
   - Last top-up date and amount
   - Quick action buttons for common tasks

2. **Quick Stats Overview** (Widget Cards)
   - **Total Credits:** Current available balance in SGD
   - **Credits Used:** Total amount spent on courses
   - **Pending Payments:** Outstanding or processing transactions
   - **Active Courses:** Number of currently enrolled courses
   - Each widget includes:
     - Icon representation
     - Numerical value
     - Percentage change indicator (compared to previous period)
     - Color-coded status (green for positive, red for negative)

3. **Recent Transactions Section**
   - Table displaying last 5-10 transactions
   - Columns: Date, Description, Type (Credit/Debit), Amount, Balance
   - Transaction status badges (Completed, Pending, Failed)
   - Quick filter buttons (All, Credits, Payments)
   - "View All Transactions" link to full history

4. **Course Enrollment Widget**
   - List of active/upcoming courses
   - Course name, institution, start date
   - Enrollment status indicator
   - Payment status for each course
   - Quick link to browse available courses

5. **Upcoming Events/Notifications Panel**
   - Important announcements from Ministry of Education
   - Upcoming course deadlines
   - Top-up schedules
   - System maintenance notices
   - Promotional offers for courses

#### 2. Pay Course Fees

**What Users Can Do:**

**Course Selection Process:**
- **Browse Approved Courses**
  - Search by course name, provider, or category
  - Filter by: Price range, Duration, Location, Start date
  - View course details: Description, fees, eligibility, duration
  - Read reviews and ratings from other users
  - Add courses to wishlist for later

- **Payment Methods**
  - Use education account balance (primary)
  - Credit/Debit card
  - PayNow
  - Bank transfer
  - Split payment (combination of methods)

- **Payment Process:**
  1. Select course from catalog
  2. Review course details and fees
  3. Choose payment method
  4. Confirm payment details
  5. Review and accept terms & conditions
  6. Submit payment
  7. Receive instant confirmation

- **Payment Features:**
  - Save payment methods for future use
  - Set up auto-payment for recurring courses
  - Schedule future payments
  - Apply promotional codes or vouchers
  - Request payment receipts (PDF/email)

- **Payment Tracking:**
  - View payment status (Processing, Completed, Failed)
  - Receive payment confirmation email
  - Track refund requests
  - Monitor course enrollment status

**How to Access:**
- Click "Pay Fees" from main navigation
- Or use "Make Payment" quick action button on dashboard

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Who is eligible for EduCredit?
**A:** Singapore Citizens aged 16-30 years old.

### Q: When is my account activated?
**A:** Your account is automatically created when you turn 16.

### Q: How do I receive credits?
**A:** Credits are automatically topped up based on government schemes. No action required from your end.

### Q: What can I use the credits for?
**A:** You can use credits to pay for approved educational courses registered in the system.

### Q: How do I check my balance?
**A:** Log in to the e-Service Portal and view your account balance on the dashboard.

### Q: Can I use other payment methods?
**A:** Yes, besides your education account balance, other payment methods are available for course fees.

### Q: How do I access my transaction history?
**A:** Navigate to the "Transaction History" section in your e-Service Portal dashboard.

### Q: Who do I contact for support?
**A:** Contact the Education Account System support team through the government helpdesk.
